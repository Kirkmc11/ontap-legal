Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2026  ·  Last updated: April 2026

The short version: On Tap collects only what's needed to run the app. We don't sell your data. You can delete everything at any time.

1. Who We Are

On Tap is operated by Kirk McMahon. Questions? Contact us at kirk@you-nameit.co.za.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Account information — name and email when you sign up or sign in with Google or Apple.
  • Drink logs — the type and timestamp of drinks you log.
  • Device preferences — settings like appearance, notifications and daily limits stored locally on your device.
  • Usage data — if you consent to tracking, anonymised data may be collected via Google AdMob for ads. You can withdraw consent anytime in iPhone Settings.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the app's core features
  • To sync your drink logs across devices via Firebase
  • To send optional push notifications you have enabled
  • To show ads to free-tier users via Google AdMob
  • To respond to support requests

4. Data Storage and Security

Drink logs and account data are stored in Google Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest. Your profile photo is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — optional sign-in. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In — optional sign-in. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — advertising for free users. Privacy Policy

6. Advertising and Tracking

On Tap shows banner ads to free users via Google AdMob. On first launch we ask permission to track your activity for personalised ads under Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You may decline — ads will still appear but won't be personalised. Change this anytime in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. Pro subscribers see no ads.

7. Your Rights

  • Access — export your drink logs as CSV from Settings → Export My Data
  • Delete — permanently delete your account and all data from Settings → Delete Account
  • Correct — update your name and email in your Profile
  • Withdraw consent — for ad tracking anytime in iPhone Settings

8. Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. On account deletion all data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. Local device data is removed when you uninstall the app.

9. Children's Privacy

On Tap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child has submitted data and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will update the date above when we do. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions? Email us at kirk@you-nameit.co.za.
